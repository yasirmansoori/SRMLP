Sophomore

College is the ideal time to learn to fail. Some things to keep in mind in second year.

  • Learn how to fail. Learn to get back up and fail again until you realise it is what eventually leads to success.
  • Study to fail in school but to rise again to learn more and become an expert.
  • Learn to fail in sports and then come back to be the best at it.
  • Learn to fail in relationships so that you may see what it truly is and return even stronger to become a better person.
  • Learn to fail while perfecting an art, but practise so diligently that it becomes second nature to you.

Junior

Every year of college is crucial in its own right, bringing something fresh to the table.Here are some things to keep in mind throughout your Junior year.

  • If you are still financially dependent on your parents, you should work on being financially independent.
  • Maintain a constant level of activity while still spending time with family and friends.
  • Take excellent care of your health. Start exercising if you aren't already. Because if you don't start now, you'll never start.
  • If you are addicted to social media, attempt to wean yourself off gradually. Because it will never benefit you in any manner.

Senior

College is the ideal time to learn to fail. Some things to keep in mind in second year.

  • Build your career.
  • A Senior-year student is still very young and does not need to have their whole life figured out, but one should have a basic notion of the wider field they want to enter post-graduation.
  • Internships are something that every college student should explore because they not only look good on your CV but also provide valuable insight into the experience. As a senior, make this year the year you get the internship you really, truly want.
  • Just as work experience as an intern or job holder looks great on your resume, so does having worked in a position of responsibility. Look for any you can secure-be it in a society of your college or in other openings.
